About

I am currently a NIST GMSE Fellow working with Dr. William Bernstein on research to help analysts better understand and debug their analysis programs using visualization.

I recieved a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.A. in Mathematics with a concentration in statistics from the University of Montana.
