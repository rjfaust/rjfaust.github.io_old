What is Anteater?

Anteater is a debugging system that incorporates data visualization into the debugging process. It allows a user to choose variables and expressions that they wish to track throughout the execution of their Python program. Anteater instruments the program to record every instance of these variables and expressions, along with all function calls and loops. It then returns the trace file to a web page where the user can create data visualizations of the values collected in the trace.

Purpose of the Study

The purpose of this study is to validate the design of Anteater with real python users. We want to observe how real programmers use Anteater, learn what parts of Anteater they find helpful to the programming process, and understand how Anteater deviates from their expectations of a programming/debugging tool.


Your role as a participant

INnthe first session, you will be asked to bring a python program of your choosing. During the meeting, I will give you a more in depth introduction into Anteater. We will then discuss your program and determine if it is a good fit for the study. If it is, between the first and second sessions, I will create a set of tasks, specific to your program, for you to complete during the second session.

In the second session, I will give you the tasks I have created for your porgram and ask you to complete them with Anteater. These tasks will be designed to guide your exploration with Anteater and hopefully help you gain insight into your program. After you have completed the tasks, you will be given time to use Anteater more if you desire to do so. Once you have finished the tasks and free exploration, I will ask you some follow-up questions about your experiences using Anteater.
